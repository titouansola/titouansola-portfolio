Festiplanner

Festiplanner is a volunteer's shift management tool dedicated to festival organizers.

Each shift requires a certain amount of volunteers to achieve missions, but they also want to see their favorite perfomers! With Festiplanner, volunteers have now the ability to organize themselves and be assured that shifts do not overlap with a show they want to assist to.

Organizers of course will set some rules, to avoid understaffing/overstaffing issues.
